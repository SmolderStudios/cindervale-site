❔ How to Play

  • Spin. Each spin costs your bet. Match 3, 4, or 5 of a kind from the left on any payline. The paytable on the left shows every symbol's value and odds — live.
  • The 5 paylines:
    Top row Middle row Bottom row V shape Λ shape
  • The Reaper's Toll. Every 12 spins Death demands cash. The price rises 34% every time, forever — it never looks at how rich you are, only how long you have lasted. Only the Toll can kill you: going broke mid-round puts you in debt (red cash) — the House lends you spins down to the size of your next Toll. If the Toll arrives and you can't pay, the run ends.
  • Rites. Every Toll you survive, the dark pays you back: pick 1 of 3 Rites — run-permanent buffs, many of which bend the actual spawn odds on the reels. Watch the paytable percentages move as you take them.
  • Desperation. The lower your cash falls (and into debt), the luckier the reels get — up to +45% payouts plus more wilds, gold and moons. Losing makes you dangerous.
  • Underdog & Grudge (duels). Falling behind grants Underdog (up to +30% payouts and more wilds). Every hex you eat and every Toll you spend behind fills the Grudge meter — at 100 it forces a free Hex into your hand. The leader never gets to coast.
  • 🗡️ Co-op: The Hunt. Same lobby, pick The Hunt instead of Duel. You each spin your own reels and pay your own Tolls, but Scythes landing on either player's reels fill a shared Summoning meter. At full, a boss rises for both of you with a shared timer. Your winning lines wound it — and the wound is your payout multiple for that symbol, so whatever you have stacked is what you hit with: a rat-focused build genuinely mauls it with rats. Kill it for cash and a trophy card; the next one is far stronger, forever. Let it escape and it feeds on your cash. Shared fate: if either of you falls, the hunt ends — so keep your partner solvent with 💸.
  • In duels, mercy exists. Your opponent's panel has 💸 Send $50 and you have 🙏 Beg — a rich rival can bail you out of debt to keep the duel going.
  • The Night Market. After each Toll, buy card drafts — you are shown several cards and choose which to keep, so you can build toward what you already have. Cards go to your storage — play them when YOU choose: Relic PERMANENT once played · Charm ONE USE, temporary boost · Hex ONE USE, strikes your duel opponent. Playing a duplicate Relic upgrades it, up to Lv5. Unwanted cards sell for cash.
  • 🩸 Blood & the Altar. A few symbols are 🔻 marked each run (shown in the paytable). Only a winning line of a marked symbol bleeds Blood — the Altar's only currency, so it rewards committing to a symbol rather than betting big. One mark moves at every Toll. Altar offerings are infinite and climb in price; each tile shows what you own and what it currently totals, including direct reel weight buys.
  • 🗡️ Wounding a boss. Every winning line hurts it. The wound is that line's payout multiple × your multiplier for that symbol × 30 — so it comes from your BUILD, not your bet. Three rats normally hit for ~100; with Rat King at Lv5 the same line hits for ~240. While a boss is up the paytable shows a 🗡️ column with exactly what each symbol wounds for. Pairs and Cursed Gold pay cash but deal nothing.
  • 🎴 On a Hunt there is no bag. A card you take is played immediately — relics level up, charms start ticking. When you pick a card you can click it again to send it to your partner instead, and it plays on their side.
  • 🤝 Helping each other (co-op). Give cards straight to your partner from any card's popover, send them cash, and watch their panel: their marks, their Blood, their reels, every card they play and every blow they land on the boss.
  • Playing faster. AUTO spins for you (10 / 50 / ∞) and pauses itself for the market, a Rite or a duel checkpoint, then carries on once you are done. ⚡ is turbo reels. Keys: Space spin · A autospin · T turbo · 1–8 bet · Esc close / stop.
  • Trials. Long-term goals that persist across every run and pay Souls once — check them from the menu for something to chase after the basics stop scaring you.
  • 👁️ The Eye is wild — it becomes anything. 🌕 Moons charge the Blood Moon meter (watch the phase next to your streak): every Moon on screen pushes it up, and at full you get free spins with wins ×2 and your bet locked — then the meter empties and starts again. 🪙 Cursed Gold pays instantly. 🕸️ Cobwebs pay nothing.
  • 🔥 The Hellpot grows with every bet. Line up 6·6·6 to claim it all.
  • Hot streak: consecutive winning spins boost payouts. A dead spin resets it.
  • Duels: invite a friend by lobby code. You each fight your own reels — last one alive wins. Hexes sabotage them; their panel shows every card they play. Tolls are checkpoints: after your Toll you shop, then press ✔ Ready — neither player spins the next round until both are ready, so nobody races ahead while you browse. Their reels mirror live in your panel.
  • Death is progress. You keep Souls for permanent Grimoire pacts, and every card you pull joins your Collection forever.
