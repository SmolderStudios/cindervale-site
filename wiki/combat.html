Combat

Cindervale's combat runs on six stats (Magic and Ranged are on the way). Damage types and weaknesses decide how hard each swing lands.

⚔️ Attack

Your accuracy — the chance each swing lands.
Each Attack level adds +1.5 accuracy; gear atk adds +3.5 per point.
Hit chance = your accuracy ÷ (your accuracy + foe Defence). Lose the roll and you hit 0 (a miss).

💪 Strength

Raises your maximum damage roll.
Each Strength level adds +1.6 max hit; gear str adds +5 per point.
When a hit lands, damage = 50–100% of your max hit.

🛡️ Defence

Reduces how often foes connect with you.
Each Defence level adds +1.2; gear def adds +1 per point.
Foe hit chance = foe attack ÷ (foe attack + your defence).

❤️ Hitpoints

Your health pool. Reach 0 and you retreat.
Max HP = Hitpoints level × 10 + ½ × gear def (Lv 10 = 100, Lv 99 = 990).
Gains XP from every point of damage you deal, and a share of every kill.
Starts at level 10.

Damage types & weakness

Each weapon deals stab, slash, or crush damage. Monsters have a weakness (shown in the Bestiary) — matching a foe's weakness lands harder. Check each zone's foes and bring the right weapon.
