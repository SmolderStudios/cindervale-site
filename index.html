What awaits you

A world that grows without you

Set your path, close the tab, and return to spoils. Cindervale runs deep — every system feeds the next.

12 Skilling Disciplines

Woodcutting, mining, fishing, smithing, alchemy, farming, jeweler and more — each with its own resources, tools, and rhythm.

Deep Melee Combat

Six combat stats, weapon tiers, shields and two-handers, auto-eat and boss fights across a dozen escalating zones.

98-Point Passive Trees

Every single skill unfolds into a sprawling passive tree. Plus a 37-node Combat Mastery web to shape your fighting style.

Pets & Familiars

Nine zone-bound familiars stalk the shadows as ultra-rare spawns. Hunt them down to complete your collection.

Raids & Gems

Raid gauntlets, socketable combat gems, enchanting, achievements — endgame depth that keeps unfolding.

Idle by Design

Progress banks while you're gone. Come back to a richer inventory, higher levels, and new frontiers to conquer.

About us

Forged by Smolder Studios

Cindervale Idle is the work of a solo developer with a love for the tick-tick-tick of a great incremental game and the brooding weight of dark fantasy.

It's built the old way: a single, hand-crafted game with no microtransactions and no pay-to-win — just systems that reward curiosity and patience. Every monster, item, and zone wears a custom-drawn icon, and every update is shaped hand-in-hand with the community.

It's launching on Steam as a one-time purchase — buy it once and every future update arrives free. Your feedback lands directly with the developer, and the road ahead is long: magic and ranged combat, new zones, and much more are all on the horizon.
