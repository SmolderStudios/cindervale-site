Reference images for the SLOTROOM asset board.

Drop a PNG or JPG here named after the asset's filename and the matching
card on /assets/ will show it automatically (PNG is tried first, then JPG):

    prisoner.png        -> shows on the "Prisoner" card
    cabinet.png         -> shows on the "Slot machine cabinet" card
    charm_waxseal.png   -> shows on the "Wax Seal" card

These are just visual references for Troper (concept art, or a screenshot of
the current in-game placeholder with the note "make it look like this, but a
real model"). They are NOT the deliverables — the deliverables are the .glb
files, which go in the shared folder, not here.
