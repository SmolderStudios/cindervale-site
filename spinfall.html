❔ How to Play

  • Spin. Each spin costs your bet. Match 3 on any of the 5 lines (3 rows + 2 diagonals) to win. Cherries pay on a left pair too.
  • The House Cut. Every 10 spins the House demands cash — and it always wants more. Can't pay? Run over. Go broke? Run over.
  • The Shop. After each Cut, buy card packs. Cards are passive bonuses in 5 rarities. Duplicates level them up (max Lv 3), spares refund cash.
  • 🃏 Wild substitutes any symbol. ⭐ 3+ Stars anywhere = free spins with doubled wins. 🪙 Coins pay instantly. 💀 Skulls pay nothing… usually.
  • Jackpot Pot grows with every bet. Line up 7·7·7 to claim it all.
  • Hot streak: consecutive winning spins boost payouts. A dead spin resets it.
  • Death is progress. Runs end. You keep Tokens — spend them on permanent upgrades — and every card you pull is recorded in your Collection forever.
